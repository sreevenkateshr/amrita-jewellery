About Me

I am Murali, a goldsmith with over 25 years of experience in crafting exquisite gold and silver jewelry. Based in Nagercoil, Kanyakumari, I specialize in all kinds of gold and silver works, including traditional designs and custom creations. Additionally, I offer beautiful Malai collections and serve customers across India. My passion for this craft and commitment to quality have been the cornerstone of my career.
